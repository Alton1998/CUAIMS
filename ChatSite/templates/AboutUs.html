About Us

Our lives would be made so much better if we were to make all these things that surround us smarter and well connected.Christ university water module aka “THE TIP-DROP PROJECT” aims for the exact same thing. How easy it would be if we could monitor and control the water level and supply just at the tip of our fingers rather than physically going and doing the same .The project is aimed at delivering premium experience to the users all concerned with the same. This uphill task of monitoring this is taken care of by four aspiring individuals of Christ University Faculty of Engineering namely Alton Lavin D’Souza , Jithin Antony Joseph , Amith Sankar Sunil Lal and Dylan Philip Jose guided by Rahul B. George,Ashish Stanley .The project is mainly directed towards helping the users to know the water level at each block , each floor , the sumps and the reservoirs at any given point of time . All of this has been meticulously incorporated into the website providing world class user experience with utmost security and care.
